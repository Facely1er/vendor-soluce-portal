## üèóÔ∏è Platform Architecture

### **Core Product: Vendor Portal Assessments**

VendorTal‚Ñ¢ Risk Review provides a comprehensive vendor portal assessment solution organized around 3 key pillars:

#### **Pillar 1: Vendor Risk Assessment & Intelligence** ‚≠ê

**Purpose:** Understand and quantify vendor risks before engagement

**Features:**
- **VendorIQ** (`/tools/vendor-iq`) - 4-module comprehensive assessment system
  - Dashboard: Portfolio overview and risk trends
  - Assessment: Multi-dimensional risk scoring (6 dimensions)
  - Data Management: Import/export vendor data
  - NIST Mapping: C-SCRM control mapping to NIST 800-161

- **Risk Radar** (`/tools/vendor-risk-radar`) - Privacy-focused vendor risk analysis
  - 6 risk dimensions: Data Sensitivity, Access Control, Data Residency, Retention, Encryption, Compliance
  - Radar chart visualization
  - Category-specific templates (Cloud Storage, Payment Processors, HR Software, etc.)
  - GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA, PCI-DSS, SOX compliance mapping

- **Vendor Risk Calculator** (`/tools/vendor-risk-calculator`) - Quick risk scoring tool

**Value:** Prevent high-risk vendor relationships before they create security/compliance issues

---

#### **Pillar 2: Continuous Vendor Management** ‚≠ê

**Purpose:** Monitor and manage vendor portfolio throughout lifecycle

**Features:**
- **Vendor Dashboard** (`/vendors`) - Complete vendor lifecycle management
  - Risk level categorization (Low, Medium, High, Critical)
  - Compliance status tracking
  - Real-time risk scoring
  - Risk distribution and trend analysis

- **Vendor Security Assessments** (`/vendor-security-assessments`)
  - NIST SP 800-161 assessments
  - CMMC Level 1/2 assessments
  - Custom framework support
  - Assessment distribution to vendors
  - Response tracking and analytics

- **Vendor Assessments** (`/vendor-assessments`)
  - Automated security questionnaire distribution
  - Response management
  - Compliance gap analysis
  - Remediation tracking
  - **[Demo Portal](/vendor-assessments-demo)** - Interactive demo of the vendor assessment experience

**Value:** Maintain ongoing visibility into vendor security posture

---

#### **Pillar 3: Asset-Vendor Relationship Intelligence** ‚≠ê

**Purpose:** Understand how vendors impact your critical assets

**Why Asset Management Matters:**
- **Risk Context:** Vendor risk varies dramatically by business impact. A vendor managing non-critical internal documentation is very different from a vendor handling your customer payment processing system.
- **Data Governance:** Track which vendors access sensitive data (PII, PHI, financial data)
- **Compliance Mapping:** Different assets have different compliance requirements (HIPAA for PHI, PCI-DSS for payment data)
- **Business Impact:** Understanding asset criticality helps prioritize vendor risk remediation efforts

**Features:**
- **Asset-Vendor Dashboard** (`/asset-vendor-dashboard`)
  - Mapping of assets to vendors
  - Relationship criticality assessment
  - Data access level tracking
  - Integration type documentation (API, Database, File Transfer, etc.)
  - Contract lifecycle management

- **Asset Management** (`/asset-management`)
  - Software, hardware, service, data, infrastructure inventory
  - Criticality classification
  - Business impact assessment
  - Data classification (Public, Internal, Confidential, Restricted)
  - Compliance requirements tracking

- **Asset-Vendor Relationship Manager**
  - Link assets to vendors
  - Define relationship types (Primary, Secondary, Support, Licensing, Maintenance)
  - Track data access levels
  - Document security requirements per relationship
  - Due diligence requirement automation

**Value:** Understand vendor dependencies and assess third-party risk impact on critical assets
